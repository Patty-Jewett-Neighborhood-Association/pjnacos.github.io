Membership
PJNA is a 501(c)(3) all-volunteer organization.

All residents/property owners in the Patty Jewett Neighborhood are welcome to join.

Why Join PJNA?

A $20 annual donation supports activities that define our neighborhood. Fill out the below form to become a member today!

  • Help build community and improve our neighborhood
  • Receive emails about neighborhood happenings, volunteer opportunities, & current events
  • Develop & participate in community events (art projects, porchfest, garage sale, etc.)
  • Participate in Peppermint Patties (kids play group) & the Neighborhood Food Project
  • Meet your neighbors
